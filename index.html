Privacy Policy - StreakXP Trading Trainer

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StreakXP Trading Trainer handles user data.

Data We Collect

The app primarily stores gameplay and profile data locally on your device.

  • Local profiles
  • XP, levels, streaks, missions, challenge progress
  • Trade history and local app preferences

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. Purchase history may be processed for app functionality (unlocking/restoring PRO access).

We do not collect payment card details directly in the app.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Data Deletion

You can delete data directly in the app:

  • Delete single profile from the Profile screen
  • Use Reset totale dati locali for full local reset

See detailed steps at /privacy/delete-data/.

Security

Data transmitted off-device for billing-related functionality is protected in transit using industry-standard encryption.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, use the support contact shown on the Google Play listing for StreakXP Trading Trainer.
